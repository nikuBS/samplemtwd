WHAT IS MIRI?

A new way to pay your phone bills!
Top up your MIRI in advance and have your mobile fees,
device installments and mobile payments automatically
deducted from this balance

Benefits of Using MIRI

Pre-pay your phone bills, while receiving
post-paid benefits

  • Choose between a Flexible Plan Contract Discount and Standard Device Subsidy
  • Choose your desired post-paid plan
  • Enjoy a variety of benefits including FLO, T membership etc.

Benefits of Using MIRI

Pay the same way as you did in your home country

  • Pay your phone bills in advance, all at once
  • No more visiting the store every month to pay your phone bills
  • No more worrying about your phone bills when not in Korea

Use MIRI to Pay For

  • Telco Services

  • Paid Mobile Content

How To Top Up with MIRI

SK Telecom Stores Credit/Debit cards, SK pay, Cash

Customer Call Center Credit/Debit cards, SK pay, Bank Account Transfer

  • Bank Account Transfer : account holder and owner of the phone line must be the same person
  • SK pay : SK pay account must be registered under the same owner as the phone line
  • No cumbersome sign-ups required! Just call the SK Telecom customer call center (114 for SK Telecom subscribers) or visit the nearest SK Telecom T world store to top up MIRI
  • Top up anytime you like, or when your balance runs out

Thins to Note

  • When the balance runs out, payment will be automatically switched to the pre-registered post-paying method
  • Unused balance will be carried over to the following month
  • Unused balance is not refundable; refunds can only be made when the phone line is terminated
  • Multiple-line/bundled products and wired (landline, internet) services must be topped up individually
  • Minimum Top up Amount : ₩10,000, Top up Limit : ₩5,000,000
