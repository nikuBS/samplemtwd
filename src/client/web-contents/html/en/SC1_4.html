REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

Main contents

View the documents required for
foreign residents to sign up

All foreign residents need to have a bank
account in Korea to sign-up

  • Self

    • Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration (Pre-paid Services: Certificate of alien registration, alien registration card or passport)

    Proxy

    • Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration of the applicant
    • Proxy representative’s valid ID (Pre-paid Services: Certificate of alien registration or passport and Proxy representative’s valid ID)

  • Self

    • Diplomatic ID
    • Passport

    Proxy

    • Diplomatic ID
    • Passport
    • Proxy representative’s valid ID

  • Self

    • US Armed Forces in Korea ID card or Certificate of service/station order
    • International driver's license

    Proxy

    • US Armed Forces in Korea ID card or Certificate of service/station order
    • International driver's license
    • Proxy representative’s valid ID
    • You may sign up at stores or branches exclusively for foreign residents (For pre-paid services, either an ID card or international driver’s license will be sufficient)

  • Self

    • Overseas Korean Resident Card (Alien Registration Card) or Certificate of Overseas Korean Registration

    Proxy

    • Overseas Korean Resident Card (Alien Registration Card) or Certificate of Overseas Korean Registration
    • Proxy representative’s valid ID

  • Self

    • Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration

    Proxy

    • Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration of the applicant
    • Proxy representative’s valid ID

  • Self

    • Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration of the minor
    • Certificate of Seal Impression of the minor’s legal guardian
    • Subscription consent form
    • Proof of family relations (issued by Korea Immigration Services)

    Legal Guardian
    (less than 14 years of age)

    • Proof of family relations (issued by Korea Immigration Services)
    • Subscription consent form
    • Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration of the minor’s legal guardian

    Legal Guardian
    (14 years of age or older)

    • Proof of family relations (issued by Korea Immigration Services)
    • Subscription consent form
    • Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration of the minor
    • All family members should be registered under the same address on the proof of family relations document issued by the Korean Immigration Service
    • An Alien Registration Card or Certificate of Alien Registration is only issued to minors 14 years of age or older
    • Foreign minors who are married to Korean nationals will be subjected to the same requirements as Korean minors when providing proof of relations with a National Health Insurance registration certificate
