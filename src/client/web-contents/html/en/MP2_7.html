Unlimited Talktime

  • The use of monthly plan may be restricted under the following cases
    • When daily talktime exceeds 600 mins more than 3 times per month
    • When the total monthly talktime exceeds 10,000 mins
    • When used for commercial purposes including tele-marketing/phone dating services etc
  • When usage is restricted, a talktime usage restriction alert message will be sent to the customer, and after which talktime charges will be incurred based on actual call duration (When used for commercial purposes, the subscriber’s plan will be automatically changed to the Standard Plan and charged accordingly)

Text

  • SMS, MMS (Text-type, Multimedia-type)
  • The use of mobile services and the monthly plan may be restricted under the following cases
    • When the daily texts exceed 200 over 10 times per month
    • When used for commercial purposes including spam messages or automatic sending programs and devices
  • When usage is restricted, a ‘packaged talktime usage restriction’ alert message will be sent to the customer, and talktime charges will be incurred based on actual calling time after
  • For subscribers whose job requires large volumes of text messaging (couriers, call-taxi drivers, credit card delivery personnel, substitute drivers etc.), exceptions can be made through a separate application process
    • How to apply: Submit a copy of employment certificate and employee card to the SK Telecom customer call center (114) or visit an SK Telecom store. Needs to be renewed every 6 months
    • Other than the above cases, the daily text amount is restricted to 500 texts according to the Anti-Spam Measures by the Korea Communications Commission

Discounts

  • The subscriber may sign up for ‘Family Plan’ / ‘Family Free’ bundled discount plans (Can be recognized as a Family group line, current ‘Family Complimentary’ subscribers can also sign up)
  • ‘Premium Customer Discounts’ not available
  • ‘Flexible Plan Contract Discounts’ can be applied for
  • ‘T We-Family’ discounts can be applied (If the accumulated use period of all family members exceeds 20 years, 10% discount is applied to the monthly fee, 30% discount for 30 years)
  • ‘Fixed Plan Contract Discounts’ cannot be applied (Current subscribing customers can continue to remain signed up for this discount plan but will not receive monthly fee discounts)
  • ‘Long-term Subscriber Discount’ and ‘Rainbow Point’ accumulation services not available

Benefits

  • Upon subscribing to ‘0 Plan Large’, the ‘VIP membership’ option will be automatically selected, and can be subsequently changed to the other options if desired
  • Mandatory to choose 1 out of the 3 available options; choosing 2 or more options is not allowed
  • Changing among the 3 add-on services of a maximum of 2 times will be allowed on the day of subscription; subsequent changes may only be made once a month

T Membership Benefits

  • Members subscribing to the plan between the 1st and 15th day, and between the 16th and last day of each month are automatically upgraded to VIP on the 1st day and 16th day of following month respectively (the line must not be suspended at any time from the point of registration till the date of membership upgrade)
  • During the upgrading period, no changes must be made to the VIP membership or mobile plan. When the VIP membership option is cancelled, the subscriber’s membership level will be downgraded the following month
  • Free ‘Loss & Damage Insurance’ under the VIP membership benefits is only offered to subscribers with monthly fees of KRW 75,000 (VAT included) and above; therefore ‘0 Plan Large’ subscribers (KRW 69,000/month) do not qualify for this offer

Free FLO and Data

  • Free mobile streaming music service FLO + addition 3GB of FLO-only data/month
  • After selecting the FLO and Data option, a text message with instructions on how to activate the service will be sent to the subscriber along with an activation URL (http://www.music-flo.com/purchase/skt). The subscriber must manually activate the FLO pass through this link. (The FLO pass must be activated for the subscriber to enjoy this service. Existing FLO and Data users will not receive this text message and instead receive a discount on usage fees calculated on a pro-rata basis.)
  • Usage fees will be discounted starting from the point in time in which the FLO and Data option was selected. (Subscribers who are not already subscribed to the FLO and Data add-on service will be automatically subscribed to the service upon selection of this option)
  • When changing to the other 2 available options, the subscriber will be charged for using FLO and Data
  • Subscribers who are underaged minors can choose this option after proving the approval of their legal guardians through the customer call center or at SK Telecom stores

  • Free ‘Broadcast Package [Broadcast(LIVE+VOD) + 10 VOD downloads]’
  • Broadcast Package: HD Video can be watched on mobile+PC, 1 simultaneous user
  • Additional 1GB/day wavve-only data (Speeds restricted to 3Mbps after 1GB/day)
  • After selecting the wavve and Data option, a text message with instructions on how to activate the service will be sent to the subscriber along with an activation URL (http://member.wavve.com/skt). The subscriber must manually activate the wavve pass through this link. (The wavve pass must be activated for the subscriber to enjoy this service. Existing wavve and Data users will not receive this text message and instead receive a discount on usage fees calculated on a pro-rata basis.)
  • Usage fees will be discounted starting from the point in time in which the wavve and Data option was selected. (Subscribers who are not already subscribed to the wave and Data add-on service will be automatically subscribed to the service upon selection of this option)
  • When changing to the other 2 available options, the subscriber will be charged for using wavve and Data
  • Subscribers who are underaged minors can choose this option after proving the approval of their legal guardians through the customer call center or at SK Telecom stores

Fees and Charges

  • When Commercial/Video Call limits are exceeded, KRW 1.98/sec for voice (including HD Voice and Commercial call) and KRW 3.3/sec for video will be charged respectively
  • Text use is limited according to the Fair Use Policy and will be charged at SMS KRW 22/text, text-type MMS KRW 33/text, multimedia-type MMS KRW 110/text
  • Subscribers who would like to use data at original speeds after finishing their monthly packaged data may do so according to the following data rates:(Only applicable to ‘0 Plan Small’, ‘0 Plan Medium’ subscribers)

Extra Data Rates: KRW 0.011/0.5KB below 880MB, KRW 19,800 flat rate over 880MB below 3GB, no extra charge for over 3GB (However speeds will be restricted to 200Kbps. Requesting to lift restrictions will result in charges of KRW 0.0033/0.5KB used.)
