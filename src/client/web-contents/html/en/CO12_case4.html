  • What is Simple Log-in?

    Simple Log-in service allows you to log in by mobile phone verification, without needing to log in with your ID/passcode

    Customers with an SK Telecom mobile phone subscribed under their own name may use the simple log-in service

    This service is also available for customers who have signed up for “corporate line personal verification service” with their own name

    If the entered information is incorrect!

    Please check whether the SK Telecom subscriber is the same person as the mobile phone user. If you have enteredthe information other than that of the SK Telecom subscriber, please try again
